Tower Defense

📖 How to Play

🎯 Objective

Defend your base by placing towers along the enemy path. Don't let enemies reach the end!

🏗️ Towers

  • Archer Tower — Fast attacks, moderate damage
  • Cannon Tower — Slow but powerful splash damage
  • Ice Tower — Slows enemies down
  • Lightning Tower — Chain lightning hits multiple enemies
  • Sniper Tower — Extreme range, high single-target damage

🎮 Controls

  • Click a tower from the shop, then click an empty cell to place it
  • Click an existing tower to upgrade or sell it
  • Press Start Wave to begin each wave
  • Press ⏩ to toggle fast-forward
